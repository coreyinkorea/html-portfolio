Three movies I like.

If we have similar tastes, you will like these movies, too.

Before The Devil Knows You're Dead

A funny suspense movie about a ne'er-do-well who cooks up a half-baked plan for a robbery with his brother. Starring Ethan Hawke, Philip Seymour Hoffman, and Marisa Tomei. Directed by the late, great Sidney Lumet. (2007)

Uncut Gems

Adam Sandler stars as a Jewish gem dealer caught in the throes of a gambling addiction. How will he get out of the mix-up he's in with an NBA star and a couple of Irishmen from the wrong side of the tracks? Directed by the Safdie brothers. (2019)

Triple Frontier

Join a team of ex-special forces commandos as they travel through South America carrying millions of dollars they stole from a cartel leader. Starring Ben Affleck and Oscar Isaac. Directed by J.C. Chandor. (2019)
